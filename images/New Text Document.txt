In game, press p to pause game and r to resume game.
Press F1 to save the current game and return to main menu. The save game can be resumed from the main menu by clicking the "Continue" button.
Drag the basket or press left and right arrows to move the basket.
White egg adds 1 point, Blue egg adds 5 points and golden egg adds 10 points. Catching shit deducts 10 points.
Three types of perks can also be dropped - time increment, bigger basket and 2X score-factor.
Completing a game or starting a new game erases the previously saved game.
